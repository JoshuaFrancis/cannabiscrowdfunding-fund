Who participates

For retail investors who want cannabis exposure and operators who need growth capital.

Canadian investors who want to participate in the cannabis sector without purchasing public market equities. Crowdfunding exemption allows non-accredited participation with amounts that fit a diversified personal portfolio alongside other asset classes.

Licensed operators

Cannabis producers, processors, and retailers holding a Health Canada licence who need growth capital between institutional rounds. Platform fundraising provides working capital without the dilution pressure of venture terms and builds a loyal retail investor base.
